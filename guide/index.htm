Overview

This page is documentation for the interaction prototype for a new School of Design website, available at this site. The intent of this page is to highlight insights that we have learned over the course of a semester researching the school and its key constituencies, as well as document the various interaction and visual standards that we have established and the thought processes behind their inception. At the bottom of the page is a diagram of which pages are presently functional, and thusly, said diagram may change over time.
