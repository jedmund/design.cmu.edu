Programs

The School of Design offers a full range of education, from high school precollege all the way to continuing education as a working professional. We believe that design is an integral part of modern life, and the sooner people are exposed to it, the sooner it can help them solve the problems they face every day.

Precollege

Spend a summer at Carnegie Mellon as a high school junior or senior.

Undergraduate

Our nationally-acclaimed four year BFA program.

  • BFA in Communication Design
  • BFA in Industrial Design

Graduate

Continue education and apply design to your expertise.

  • MDes in Comm. Planning and Info Design
  • MDes in Interaction Design
  • Master of Product Development

Continuing Ed

Learn design principles to improve your business.

  • Executive Education
