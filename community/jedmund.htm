Justin Edmund

BFA in Communication Design, Senior www.jedmund.com

Justin Edmund is a designer and developer graduating from the the Carnegie-Mellon University School of Design in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He specializes in product and interaction design, and is interested in social information and how it can be used to educate users about the passive and hidden details of their everyday life.

He strives to use human-driven design and social media as a platform for cultural research, and one day hopes to change the world with design.
