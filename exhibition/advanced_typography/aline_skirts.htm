Advanced Typography / Aline Skirts Fall 2010

Aline skirts, a brand under Kelly Lane Design, are skirts made from refurbished fabrics. The customer can customize the fabric choices for her skirt which is also reversible. Branding and design focused around the versatility of the skirts and the unique creativity that the A-Line woman can bring to designing their own piece.
