What is Communication Design?

We believe design is central to a better future for people and the planet. In Communication Design, we focus on developing effective and appropriate forms of communication through the study and practice of design principles, theories and skills.

Students graduate with a BFA in Communication Design, and many are working in companies as varied as Siegel+Gale, Lippincott, Apple, Google, Microsoft, and Johnson & Johnson.

The required communication design studio courses introduce principles of communication with type and image in both static and dynamic forms. Students take a sequence of typography studios as well as color and digital imaging classes.

Alumni and Careers

Facebook is a social utility that connects people with friends and others who work, study and live around them.

Facebook Design creates the visual elements of Facebook, and also writes the code to build those designs. They conduct research to gain user insights, and use those results to make products as intuitive, simple and elegant as possible.

R/GA is creative agency for the digital age. They specialize in award-winning creative for digital, mobile, retail, and out-of-home experiences, and complex technology development and solutions.

R/GA houses designers of all strands, from data visualization to interaction design, visual design to motion. Designers craft solutions to the problems presented by clients like Nike, L'Oréal, and Nokia.

Johnson and Johnson is a global American pharmaceutical, medical devices and consumer packaged goods manufacturer founded in 1886.

The in-house design team at Johnson and Johnson work on branding and product strategy, design research, and develop and design internal initiatives for the company.
