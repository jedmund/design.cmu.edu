Overview

Over the past five months, we have done extensive research, iterated many concepts and developed a direction for the School of Designâ€™s web presence. We believe that embracing the modern web is one of the best things that the School can do as a brand today, and we hope that this interaction prototype makes this as clear to you as it is to us.

In the initial phase of the studio, roughly the first half of the semester, we did extensive research around the online presences of various educational and design-centric institutions. Moving forward, our goal was to create something unique to any of the websites that we came across in our research so as to re-establish the School of Design as an educator and innovator in the industry.

We believe that this School has many unique characteristics that cannot be duplicated by our much larger competitors, such as RISD or MICA. We've created a website that caters to our unique needs as an institution, playing to these natural advantages while keeping in mind a lot of the considerations that arose in both in-class discussions as well as faculty meetings, as we've heard them.
